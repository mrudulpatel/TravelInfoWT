Rome

Rome is the capital and largest city of Italy. It is known for its rich history, stunning architecture, and delicious food. Visitors can explore iconic landmarks such as the Colosseum, the Pantheon, and the Trevi Fountain. They can also enjoy delicious Italian cuisine in one of the city's many restaurants or cafes.

Top Attractions

  • The Colosseum
  • The Pantheon
  • The Roman Forum
  • The Vatican Museums
  • The Trevi Fountain
  • The Spanish Steps
  • The Castel Sant'Angelo
  • The Piazza Navona
