India is a diverse and vibrant country located in South Asia. It is the seventh-largest country in the world and has a population of over 1.417 billion people, making it the first populous country.India is known for its beautiful architecture, ancient temples, and colorful festivals such as Diwali, Holi, and Navratri. The country is also famous for its spicy and flavorful cuisine, which varies from region to region. Some of the most popular tourist destinations in India include the Taj Mahal, Jaipur, Goa, Kerala, and the Himalayan region.
